Ten amazing facts about Goodfellas!

Read time: 3 minutes

1. Goodfellas was released in 1990.

2. Goodfellas had an all-time ensemble cast, including Robert De Niro, Joe Pesci and Ray Liotta.

3. Goodfellas was the 12th movie Martin Scorsese directed and the 6th collaboration between him and legendary actor Robert De Niro.

4. Goodfellas is based on the 1985 Nicholas Pileggi book 'Wiseguys'

5. Goodfellas was nominated for 6 Oscars, only winning 1 with Joe Pesci taking Best Supporting Actor.

6. Goodfellas used the f-word 300 times, making it the 16th most f-bomb-laden movie ever released.

7. Tony Lip is the only actor to appear in both Goodfellas and the TV Show 'The Sopranos'.

8. In order to get into character, Ray Liotta listened to FBI tapes of Henry Hill on his way to set everyday.

9. Despite being a movie renowned for violence, it only has a body count of 10.

10. Al Pacino had the opportunity to play Jimmy Conway but turned it down due to not wanting to be typecast.
